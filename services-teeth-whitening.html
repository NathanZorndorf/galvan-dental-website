Teeth Whitening


Take years off your smile! Did you know that whiter teeth exude youthfulness and confidence? Our lifestyle and age contribute to the staining and darkening or yellowing of our teeth. In today’s world, everybody wants to look younger and healthier. A whiter smile makes a lasting impression that can open doors for you both professionally and personally.

The remedy to that is teeth whitening. Our office clinic provides several professional strength options for those who want a whiter and more youthful smileat different affordable price points.

Before considering teeth whitening, it is crucial to know that teeth whitening only affects the natural tooth enamel, it is important to evaluate replacement of any old fillings, crowns, etc. Replacement of any restorations will be done after bleaching so they will match the newly bleached teeth.

Types of whitening we offer:

Zoom! Teeth Whitening:

This is the a rapid, single visit teeth whitening treatment that is done in our office. In about an hour and a half, you will be able to achieve the whiter teeth you desire. Sensitivity is normal, and it will subside in a few hours. Our staff will give you instructions on what foodss to temporarily avoid after the treatment. It is sometimes necessary to come back for a retouch or maintenance to keep teeth at their whitest.



Custom Take Home Trays:

Our office also offers the customized take home whitening kits. These can be done in just two visits. On the initial visit, we will take an impression where a cast will be made of your upper and lower arch. Then trays will be made from these casts. On your next visit, the doctor will explain to you how to use the trays and the whitening gel at home. This process takes more time. It could be days before you see the result. Custom trays work just as well as the Zoom whitening. Like any professional grade whitening, your teeth can be really sensitive, but this should be temporary.
